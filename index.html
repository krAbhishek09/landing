Why is my computer running slow

Slow performance can be caused by various factors such as malware, insufficient RAM, fragmented hard drive, or outdated software.

How can I protect my computer from viruses and malware

Utilize reputable antivirus software, avoid suspicious websites and downloads, and keep your operating system and applications.

How often should I clean my computer

It's recommended to clean your computer internally and externally every few months to prevent dust buildup and ensure proper airflow.
